Setup Instructions

1
On your Fire TV, go to Settings > My Fire TV > Developer Options and enable "Install unknown apps" for the Downloader app (or Silk Browser).
2
Open the Downloader app (free from Amazon App Store) and enter this page's URL. Or use the Silk Browser to navigate here.
3
Tap "Download & Install" above. When prompted, tap Install.
4
Launch the app. It will display a registration code (e.g. ABC123).
5
In the admin panel, go to Devices and enter the code to register. Then assign content — it appears on the TV within seconds.
